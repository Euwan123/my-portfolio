About Me

Hey there! I'm Euwan Gabriel B. Abogadie, currently a student at Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao, section A221. I've always been fascinated by how software can make life easier, which is why I got into programming in the first place.

What really excites me is creating applications that not only work well but also look good and feel intuitive to use. Most of my work revolves around C# development in Visual Studio 2022, where I enjoy building Windows Forms applications with polished GUIs. I also work extensively with Java in IntelliJ IDEA, creating robust applications that solve real problems.

I'm the kind of person who gets genuinely excited about making buttons look just right and tweaking layouts until everything feels natural. Whether it's a school project or something I'm working on in my free time, I always try to balance functionality with thoughtful design. I'm still learning and growing as a developer—but that's what makes it fun!
